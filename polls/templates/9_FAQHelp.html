FAQ / HELP

 

Preamble: IROS On-Demand is a front-end interface. It pulls content that authors submitted to PaperPlaza. Thus the paper's title, digest slide, authors' names and affiliations, are copied verbatim as the authors entered it in PaperPlaza. Also, like most web-based content, IROS On-Demand uses a browser's embedded video player to play the video. These videos should comply to HTML5 protocol (and hence employ Video: H.264 CODEC, Audio: MP3/AAC CODEC). Lastly, Paper Plaza employs globally-distributed edge servers. This network structure allows content to be "cached" to a server nearest you and those in the same region.

Q1. My video doesn't play (e.g. black screen, no audio, etc)

A1. Unlike PDFs, videos are not checked for compliance by PaperPlaza. After beta-testing the 1400+ videos, we discovered about 20% failed compliance; they did not have either the H264 CODEC for Video or the MP3/AAC CODEC for Audio. For these, we updated them with this CODEC and uploaded it to PaperPlaza. We apologize for not catching your video during our beta-test. It would be helpful if you provide the following info:

a. Paper# and Title

b. Process the video you had submitted to PaperPlaza with the HTML5 Compliant CODEC

- Free video converter for the CODEC conversion(Freeconvert - Video Converter)
- Select "MP4" for the file type
- Find the video option "Video Codec", then select "H.264"
- By the default, Audio Codec will be automatically converted AAC Codec
- Click "Convert To MP4"

c. Once the conversion is done, download to your computer
- (Important) Name your video as "Attachment_(Submission Number)_PV.mp4"
- (Important) For Workshops/Tutorials name"Attachment_(Submission Number)_WV.mp4"

d. Deposit your processed video on your own Google Drive or DropBox

e. Email info@iros2020.org "A" and video filename, and shareable link from "C"

We aim to update IROS On-Demand with any received emails and updated videos within 72-hours.

Q2. Why does it take a long time (e.g. more than 10-sec) for a video to play?

A2. Given the preamble, it's likely you were the first one in your edge server's region, to click on the video. That should be a one-time delay; once the video caches into your region's server, you should not experience delays.

Q3. There is/are typo(s)/error(s) in On-Demand e.g. mis-spelling of paper title, author's name, institution, etc

A3. Given the preamble, On-Demand pulls content verbatim, as entered by the author, from PaperPlaza. Only author's can edit their information; we don't have their PIN, etc. During beta-test, we did our best to catch these verbatim typos and reflect them in On-Demand correctly. We apologize for not catching these typo(s)/error(s) during beta-testing. It would be helpful if you provide the following info:

a. Email info@iros2020.org with Title "IROS On-Demand Typo Edit Request: #(Your Submission Number)"

b. Please provide information you would like to correct (e.g. Author First/Last Name, Affiliation, Paper Title, etc)

We aim to update IROS On-Demand with any received emails and updated videos within 72-hours.

Q4. Can I replace my video?

A4. We can not guarantee that we could do this replacement; it will depend on how many requests we get. Please follow the steps in "A1" above, "A1-c to A1-e". We will do our best to work with PaperPlaza to replace your video

 

For additional questions/concerns/issues, please email "info@iros2020.org"
